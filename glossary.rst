========
Glossary
========

.. glossary::

   artificial general intelligence

      A computer program with broad intellectual capabilities that are
      approximately indistinguisable from those of a typical human.

   artificial narrow intelligence

      A computer program that is an expert at a specific task such as visual
      recognition of objects or automated financial trading.

   artificial super intelligence

      A computer program with broad intellectual capabilities that are
      noticeably more advanced than those of a typical human.

   long short-term memory

      A type of recurrent neural network designed to deal with the vanishing
      gradient problem.

      * `Wikipedia <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Long_short-term_memory>`__

   machine learning

      The research and development of computer programs that learn from data
      and make useful predictions on unseen data.

      * `Wikipedia <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Machine_learning>`__
      * `Machine Learning Glossary <https://developers.google.com/machine-learning/glossary/#machine-learning>`__
